## Voice rules — apply everywhere

1. **Numbers over adjectives.** "Delivered in 14 days" beats "delivered fast." "₹13,000" beats "affordable."
2. **WhatsApp first, email second, calendar invites never.** SMEs don't book Calendly slots.
3. **GST + refund + 50% advance** said three times on every page. These are the trust pillars for Indian SMEs.
4. **No "studio for premium digital craft" language.** No "we craft", "we partner", "we obsess". Just "I build", "I ship", "I reply".
5. **Hinglish welcomed where natural** — especially in testimonials. Don't translate the Redline quote.
6. **Past-tense outcomes** with verifiable specifics (600K subs, 7-day delivery, ₹X charged) > future-tense promises.
7. **Never mention bands above ₹48,000.** Above that, refer the buyer somewhere else or quote on email — never publish it.
