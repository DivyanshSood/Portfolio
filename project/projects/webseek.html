WebSeek.ai

—Our own bet on where agencies go next. One credit, one prompt, any language — paired with Claude, GPT or Gemini. Five free credits while we iterate.

Open live site ↗ Build something similar
The brief

Most "AI website builders" produce identical, sectioned-template marketing pages. We wanted the opposite: a builder where the output is custom-coded, not assembled from blocks — and where a buyer can pick which model writes their site (Claude for taste, GPT for breadth, Gemini for speed).

The product lives at the intersection of three audiences: small businesses who can't afford a developer, designers who want a fast first draft, and our own studio — eating its own dog food on every new lead.
