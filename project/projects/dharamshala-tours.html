The briefSell packages, not hotel listings

Every Kangra travel operator runs the same template — a list of hotels with prices, a contact form nobody fills. The founders wanted to be different: they sell packages, not rooms, and most of conversion happens on WhatsApp.

The result
~1,000 packages booked through the site every month.
Stack

  • WhatsApp deep-link

What we shippedBuilt around the WhatsApp moment

Each package page ends in a one-tap WhatsApp deep link with the package pre-filled. The CMS lets the team push a new monsoon or winter itinerary without waiting on a developer. Mobile-first because 95%+ of their traffic is on phones.
