ChinkiZ Knitting Knife

—Chinki runs a 600K+ YouTube channel. We built her a direct-to-consumer storefront in custom code — no Shopify, no theme marketplace — with a video gallery that matches the ritual of her tutorials.

Open live site ↗ Build something similar
The brief

Chinki had tried two e-commerce builders before. Both fought the way her audience actually shops — long tutorial videos, then a comment, then a DM. She wanted a site where the video sits next to the buy button, not buried under it.

From her: "I did not think it could be achieved without going to e-commerce builders and only using custom code." We shipped a custom Next.js storefront with Razorpay, a video gallery that mirrors her YouTube grid, and an admin where she can add a new product the way she'd add a video — title, thumbnail, done.
