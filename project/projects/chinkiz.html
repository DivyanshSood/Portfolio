The briefWhere buy meets video

Chinki tried two e-commerce builders. Both fought the way her audience shops — long tutorial videos, then a comment, then a DM. She wanted a site where the video sits next to the buy button, not buried under it.

In her words
"I did not think it could be achieved without going to e-commerce builders and only using custom code."

— Chinki, Founder

Stack
  • Next.js
  • Razorpay
  • Custom video gallery
  • Admin panel
What we shippedA storefront that feels like her tutorials

A custom Next.js storefront with Razorpay, a video gallery mirroring her YouTube grid, and an admin panel where she adds products the way she'd add a video — title, thumbnail, done. Zero Shopify themes, zero builder lock-in, full code ownership.
