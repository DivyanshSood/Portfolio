InHimalayas

—A revenue-share booking platform for resorts and stays across the Himalayas. Built for global travellers, not domestic alone — LLM files + GEO optimisation drove a visible spike in AI-referred traffic.

Open live site ↗ Build something similar
The brief

The founder wanted a booking platform that didn't read like every other domestic OTA. The audience is global — backpackers, digital nomads, slow-travel families — and the editorial voice had to match.

We shipped Astro for content speed, layered llms.txt + structured data so AI assistants could quote the inventory accurately, and built a revenue-share back-end that the founder could update without touching code. AI-referred traffic showed a measurable lift within weeks.
